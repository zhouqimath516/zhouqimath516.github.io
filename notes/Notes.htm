Notes

1.Introduction to Partial Differential Equations.(pdf)

Notes for 2020 BICMR Summer School for Differential Geometry.This course is taught by Jianchun Chu from Northwestern University.The main reference is Elliptic partial differential equations of second order by D.Gilbarg and N.S.Trudinger.

2.几何笔记.(pdf)

此笔记为南京大学梅加强老师的流形与几何的课程笔记, 内容包含了微分流形与黎曼几何的基本概念(联络, 曲率).

3.Lecture Notes on Minimal Surfaces.(pdf)

This lecture note was taken for the class on minimal surfaces given by Professor Chao Xia (Xiamen University) in the summer school on Differential Geometry in the Hangzhou Dianzi University, July 2021. The class includes three parts, geometry of submanifolds, Bernstein theorem and Plateau problem. However, this note contains only the first two parts (The Plateau problem is just an introduction in the class).

4.Notes on K\"{a}hler Geometry.(pdf)

This note was taken for the class on Calabi-Yau Theorem in Complex Geometry given by Professor Ben Weinkove (Northwestern University) in the summer school in the USTC, July 2007.

5.The Classical Isoperimetric Inequality.(pdf)

This note summarizes the classical Isoperimetric Inequality, we apply the ABP technique and optimal transport technique to prove it.

6.Notes on Geometric Quantities of Convex Hypersurfaces and Starshaped Hypersurfaces.(pdf)

This note summarizes the geometric quantities of convex hypersurfaces and starshaped hypersurfaces and the corresponding curvature flow problem.
